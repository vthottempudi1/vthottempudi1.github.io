⚗️ RAG · Evidence Synthesis

Scientific Evidence Intelligence Platform

Given a molecule (name, SMILES, InChIKey, or CAS), retrieves open-access literature from PubMed/PMC, OpenAlex, ChemRxiv, and CrossRef — synthesizes evidence across papers, flags contradictions between sources, and verifies every claim traces to a real citation. LangGraph orchestration with a Critic/Verifier node and CRAG retry loop. Deployed to AWS ECS Fargate behind HTTPS at aiscientificlabs.com.
